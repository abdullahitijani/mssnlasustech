== Description ==

Classic Portfolio is a sophisticated choice for anyone wanting to showcase their work with elegance and professionalism. Designed for professionals seeking a timeless aesthetic, this design offers sleek and modern page layouts. It is a versatile platform for creatives looking to display their work. The theme is perfect for Portfolio, Business, Creative, Design, Photography, Agency, Studio, Freelancer, Art, Resume, Showcase, Professional, Consultant, Media, Branding. This portfolio is perfect for web developers, photographers, graphic designers, artists, writers, and all freelancers wanting to create a lasting impact on their potential clients, with clean lines and minimalist approach. Your work will take centre stage with this multipurpose theme. The intuitive layout organises your website with various sections like “Portfolio”, “About Me”, “‘Services Offered”, “Testimonials” and “Contact Me”. Your projects are organised well under the “Recent Work” page. Let your expert advice reach the world by adding a “Blog” section. Your projects are organised seamlessly and easy to go through because of the website’s easy navigation. Make your website your creative canvas by making use of adding exceptional quality photos and video using high-definition visual support. Power packed with visually appealing colour palette and typography, this Classic Portfolio theme also makes your website functional. Coming equipped with customizable features, every detail reflects your unique brand and personality. Built-in tools help you with easy updates and maintenance of the website that keeps it secure and running at an optimal speed. Classic Portfolio theme also offers SEO-friendly features and the theme is cross-platform and all-browsers compatible.With translation-ready theme, your work will reach clients beyond your geographical range. Elevate your online presence and impress potential clients with a portfolio that exudes professionalism and refinement. Discover the Classic Portfolio theme today and create a lasting impression with your professional portfolio.

== Homepage Installation ==

Step-1. Go to Dashboard -- Pages -- Add new page -- Add templates (Home Page) -- Publish it.
Step-2. Go to Dashboard -- Settings -- Reading -- Click on static page -- Select home page -- Save changes.

Header Section
Step-1. Go to Dashboard -- Appearnace -- Customize -- Theme option panel -- Manage Header Section -- Fill the contact information. -- Publish it.

Banner Section
Step-1. Go to Dashboard -- Pages -- Add new Page -- Add title, content, and featured image-- Publish it.
Step-2. Go to Dashboard -- Appearnace -- Customize -- Theme option panel -- Manage Banner Section -- Select the banner page -- Publish it.

Experience Section
Step-1. Go to Dashboard -- Appearnace -- Customize -- Theme option panel -- Manage Experience Section
 -- Fill the experience information. -- Publish it.

Footer Section
Step-1. Go to Dashboard -- Appearnace -- Customize -- Theme option panel -- Manage Footer Section -- Add copyright text -- Publish it.
